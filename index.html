About Me

David has been teaching private guitar lessons for the last 12 years in the greater Houston area. David grew up in Houston, and graduated from Houstons High School for Performing and Visual Arts. He then went on to study performance at the prestigious Berklee College of Music in Boston, MA, where he graduated with honors. While in college David toured extensively across the US and Poland with a performing group.

Since returning to Houston, David has played extensively and recorded with several worship bands. He has also played and recorded with a jazz quartet locally. In private lessons David shares his passion for music with his students and seeks to impart what he has learned in his career.
